How it works

Three steps. One visit.

01
Survey on site

Photograph the cut-out, board and route. Set a scale reference per photo. The site walk captures everything the office needs.

02
Design with checks

Drop chargers, draw cable runs, place boards and supplies. Cable sizing, volt drop and load-vs-main-fuse checks run as you draw.

03
Send the pack

Branded customer quote, engineer pack with single-line diagram, and the DNO application, exported as PDF before you leave.
